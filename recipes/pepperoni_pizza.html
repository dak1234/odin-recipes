Pepperoni Pizza

Desciption

This is a simple and yummy dish to prepare. Who doesn't love a good ol' pepperoni pizza?

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza crust
  • 1/2 can of pizza sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 package of pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Grease pizza pan
  3. Top pizza with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni
  4. Cook pizza until crust is browned and cheese is bubbly. Takes about 20 minutes.
  5. Slice and enjoy
